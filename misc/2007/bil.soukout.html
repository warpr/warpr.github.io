بالسكوت (ريمكس)

remix is surrounded by open/close parentheses, browsers seem to know that the open paren should be rendered right-to-left (i.e. mirrored) because it is surrounded by right-to-left script. The close paren however is not followed by right-to-left script, and is often rendered left-to-right, obviously this looks wrong and users will be inclined to then just substitute another open parentheses, because that will look like a close paren.

the following paragraph has the exact same content, but is forced right-to-left with a dir attribute.
